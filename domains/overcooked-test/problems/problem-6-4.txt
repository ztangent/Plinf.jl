Root Recipe:

The Sidecar
1½ ounces Cognac
1 ounce Cointreau
¾ ounce fresh lemon juice
Garnish: 1 orange twist

Shake all the ingredients with ice, then strain into a chilled coupe. Express the orange
twist over the drink, then gently rub it around the rim of the glass and place it into the
drink.
