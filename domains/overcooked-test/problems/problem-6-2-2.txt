Root Recipe: 

2½ ounces vodka
½ ounce dry vermouth
Garnish: 1 lemon twist or olive
Stir all the ingredients over ice, then strain into a chilled Nick & Nora glass. Garnish with
the lemon twist (expressed over the drink and set on the edge of the glass) or olive.
